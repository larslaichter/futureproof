Future Proof

Risk Art Technology

We are a network of people with an interest in ideas that last. A good idea, a good technology is one that sustains life. Today, many technologies and institutions we collaboratively create are doing immense harm.

Most of us are no longer safe from those harms and both isolated and unprepared for the next pandemic, drone strike or power outage. We need imagination and community to develop better tools and a better way of life.

Future Proof hosts a network of artists, scholars and technologists that tinker with ideas that will survive and scale. It provides the place, knowledge and infrastructure for members to retreat to for safety.
